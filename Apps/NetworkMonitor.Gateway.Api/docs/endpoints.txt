MonitoringJobController (/jobs)
POST	/jobs	CreateNewJob()	Creates a new job
PATCH	/jobs/{id}	UpdateJobById(Guid id)	Updates a job by its ID
Get()	/jobs/types	GetJobTypes()	Gets the job types


DevicesController (/api/Devices)
Get()	/api/Devices	GetAllDevices()	Retrieves a list of all devices and their online status
Get()	/api/Devices/{id}	GetDeviceById(int id)	Retrieves details of a specific device and its history
POST	/api/Devices/scan	TriggerNetworkScan()	Triggers a manual network scan and updates device statuses
Get()	/api/Devices/scans/history	GetScanHistory(int limit = 20)	Retrieves the history of previous network scans
